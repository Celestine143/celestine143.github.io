Our Location

We are located in Brgy. Ibabao, Cordoba, Cebu, Purok Makugihon — a spot that’s easy to find and convenient for everyone. Whether you're from Cordoba, nearby towns, or even Cebu City, our location makes it simple for you to visit us without any trouble. You don’t need to drive far or deal with heavy traffic just to pick up your rental van. We made sure to be in a place where our customers can easily stop by and start their journey right away.

Our place is perfect for both locals and visitors who want a smooth and quick start to their trip. If you're heading out for a family outing, a business trip, or just exploring Cebu, our easy-to-reach location helps you save time and enjoy your travel more. We are proud to serve you from a place that is central, friendly, and ready to help you get on the road as soon as possible.
